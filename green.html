NASCAR
Green Impact

Arguably the most impactful platform in the history of the U.S. in terms of influencing green attitudes and behaviors on a population scale.
  • NASCAR's 67 million fans are 100% more likely than non-fans to view their household as "very green"
  • NASCAR fans are always looking for ways to positively impact the environment, up from 70% more likely just years ago
  • Green consciousness in the U.S. has an unprecedented impact and continues to grow at nearly 50% annually based on survey of several thousand NASCAR fans vs non-fans.

Clean Air Tree Planting Program

  • Each race weekend, NASCAR partners with the local community to plant (10) trees per green flag waved
  • Each tree (not seedlings or saplings) that is planted offsets a Cup car's emissions for 500 miles
  • This initiative has seen over 5,000 trees planted since 2009
